Sales Enablement Playbook Excerpt - 2025-07-15

If competitor emphasizes fast deployment:
- Prepare a deployment checklist
- Produce a 2-week onboarding timeline
- Create a one-pager comparing onboarding steps

If competitor emphasizes workflow customization:
- Collect 3 workflow examples from existing customers
- Draft a "customization capabilities" explainer
- Train sales on qualifying questions about process complexity
