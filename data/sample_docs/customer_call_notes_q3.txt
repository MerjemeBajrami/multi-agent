Customer Call Notes - Q3 Summary - 2025-09-28

Common objections raised by prospects:

1. Onboarding concerns:
   - Some mid-market SaaS teams worry that enterprise-focused tools may take too long to implement.
   - Several prospects explicitly mentioned "implementation fatigue" from previous CRM migrations.

2. Customization complexity:
   - Enterprise sales teams appreciate deep workflow customization.
   - However, smaller teams feel overwhelmed by complex configuration screens.

3. Support expectations:
   - Fast response times are considered critical.
   - 24/7 support is perceived as a strong enterprise differentiator.

No quantitative churn data was discussed in these notes.
