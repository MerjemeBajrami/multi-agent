Market Landscape Report - CRM & Sales Automation - 2025

Segment Overview:
- Mid-market SaaS CRM buyers prioritize rapid deployment and low configuration overhead.
- Enterprise buyers prioritize workflow customization, compliance certifications, and support SLAs.

Growth Trends:
- Mid-market CRM adoption grew 18% YoY.
- Enterprise CRM modernization grew 11% YoY.

Decision Drivers:
Mid-market:
- Time-to-value
- Ease of onboarding
- Transparent pricing

Enterprise:
- Security certifications
- Advanced workflow logic
- Integration ecosystem depth

No regional revenue breakdowns were included in this report.
