Internal Strategy Notes - Competitive Positioning Workshop - 2025-10-05

Observations:

- AlphaCRM's emphasis on fast deployment resonates with mid-market SaaS.
- BetaSales appears stronger in highly regulated enterprise environments.
- Messaging clarity seems stronger in AlphaCRM’s onboarding narrative.

Risks:
- If BetaSales simplifies onboarding UX, it could neutralize AlphaCRM’s deployment advantage.
- If AlphaCRM does not expand enterprise support features, it may lose large deals.

Opportunities:
- Develop vertical-specific onboarding kits.
- Create ROI calculator focused on deployment time savings.
